| File | Tense | Use |
|------|-------|-----|
| Präsens.md | Present | Current actions, general truths |
| Perfekt.md | Present perfect | Spoken past (conversation) |
| Präteritum.md | Simple past | Written past (narrative) |
| Plusquamperfekt.md | Past perfect | Past before past |
| Futur-I.md | Future | Future events |
| Futur-II.md | Future perfect | Future speculation |
